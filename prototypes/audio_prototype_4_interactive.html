🧪 Interactive Card Features

🎭 Visual Transformation: Cards change appearance during audio playback with colors and animations.

🌊 Wave Animation: Ripple effects show audio is playing with layered wave patterns.

⚡ Speed Selection: Click speed dots to change pronunciation speed (slow/normal/fast).

👂 Listening State: Special orange state shows when listening mode is active.

📖 Sentence Audio: Click usage examples to hear words in context.

🎨 Hover Effects: Rich preview states reveal additional features and controls.
